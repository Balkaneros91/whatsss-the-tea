About

Who we are:

What's the Tea, founded in Stockholm back in 2015, is an organization focused on creating a safer environment for the lgbtq+ community and integrating our culture even more into today's society.

What we do:

We organize meetings and activities to share information about who we are and what we do in our community. We learn of and encourage one another to be proud of who we are. Our organization works against homophobia, biphobia and transphobia and spread positivity, care, and love within our community and beyond.

Inspiration

Inspired by the modern movement of Drag and its soul RuPaul our organization also offers drag shows every Friday in our premises in Stockholm's House of Culture, where we gag and laugh our heads off and of course share love, “cause sharing is caring hun”.
